My Interest in Computer Supported Collaborative Work (CSCW)

I am passionate about using developing software products to connect people and have always been curious to learn more about how systems/apps can be designed to improve the way we collaborate today. This is one of the reasons that motivated me to take CSCW (i.e., CSCE-672.600) in Fall 2022 at Texas A&M University.

More specifically, by taking this class, I look forward to learn more about how we use technology (apps/tools/etc.) to collaborate and important factors/metrics that determine the success of a system/app/tool that is used for collaborative work.

  • Travel or Delivery: Waze, Uber, UberEats, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart, Amazon, GrubHub
